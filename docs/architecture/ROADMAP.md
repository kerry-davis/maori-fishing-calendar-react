# Roadmap (Backlog Candidates)

### Core Functionality

*   Pepper rotation with dual-decrypt window
*   Configurable blind indexes for search on encrypted fields
*   Richer analytics (seasonal heatmaps)
*   Historical catch insights layered onto seasonal analytics
*   Optional stronger user passphrase mode (E2EE profile)
*   Offline-first image capture and progressive upload
*   Personalized watchlists for favourite locations and species

### Smart Trip Planning & Predictions

*   Predictive Trip Planner: Analyze user's historical success patterns (location + moon phase + weather + season) to suggest optimal fishing dates
*   Success Rate Predictions: "Based on your past 20 trips to this location during similar conditions, you have a 75% chance of catching fish"
*   Recommended Gear Suggestions: Based on successful gear for that location/season/conditions
*   Time-of-Day Analysis: "Your most successful fishing hours are 5-7 AM"
*   Companion Analysis: Track correlation between fishing success and number/type of companions
*   Equipment Effectiveness Trends: Track which gear performs better over time
*   ROI Tracking: Track cost per fish caught (if users add expenses)

### Location & Weather Features

*   Advanced weather integration with more detailed parameters
*   Species-specific fishing calendar based on Māori traditional knowledge
*   GPS integration for automatic location detection and nearby fishing spot discovery
*   Fishing spot rating system with community-driven insights
*   Hyperlocal tide and weather alerts with configurable thresholds
*   Offline-cached outlook of upcoming favourable fishing windows

### Location Intelligence

*   Fishing Spot Discovery: Show nearby saved locations on a map with success rates
*   Location Rating System: Rate spots (1-5 stars) for crowding, accessibility, facilities
*   Spot Comparison: Compare success rates across multiple saved locations
*   Drive Time Calculator: Show travel time to each saved location

### Alerts & Notifications

*   Fishing Opportunity Alerts: "High tide in 2 hours at your favorite spot - ideal fishing conditions predicted"
*   Weather Change Alerts: "Barometric pressure dropping - fish may be more active"
*   Seasonal Milestones: "Best kingfish season starts next week based on your past catches"
*   Advanced Weather Parameters: Extended 14-day forecast, solunar theory, barometric pressure tracking
*   Weather Pattern Recognition: "Strong northwest winds typically precede good fishing"

### Community & Social Features

*   Catch sharing functionality with photos and details
*   Fishing group coordination tools
*   Traditional knowledge integration with more Māori seasonal practices
*   Community tip exchanges highlighting real-time conditions and best practices

### Educational & Reference

*   Species Encyclopedia: Detailed info, regulations, best practices for each species
*   Fishing Challenges: Monthly/yearly goals ("Catch 10 different species this year")
*   Friend System: Compare stats with other anglers (optional, privacy-controlled)
*   Traditional Knowledge Deep Dive: Extended maramataka for different species, seasonal practices integration

### Technical Improvements

*   Enhanced data export/import and backup tools
*   Multi-device synchronization
*   Improved photo management with better organization and encryption
*   Offline data synchronization with automatic sync when connectivity returns

### Enhanced Offline Capabilities

*   Progressive Photo Upload: Capture photos offline, upload when connected
*   Offline Forecasting: Cache weather/tide data for offline viewing
*   Offline Maps: Download location maps for offline use
*   Background Sync: Automatically sync data when app is closed

### Search & Discovery

*   Advanced Search Filters: Search by date range, catch success (yes/no), moon phase, weather conditions
*   Saved Searches: Store frequently used search queries
*   Search History: Quick access to recent searches
*   Trip Similarity Finder: Find past trips with similar conditions for planning
*   Species Probability: Based on location/season/conditions, show likely species to catch

### Accessibility & Localization

*   Māori language support (Te Reo Māori localization)
*   Enhanced accessibility features (voice-over, high contrast mode)
*   Dark/light mode optimization for night-time fishing
*   Voice Navigation: Navigate modals and forms by voice
*   Larger Text Mode: Better support for older anglers
*   Color Blind Support: Alternative chart colors and patterns
